The Apartment

Your Home Above Lake Geneva

Nestled in the heart of Montreux, Le National is a beautifully appointed apartment offering sweeping views of Lake Geneva and the Alps. Whether you're here for the world-famous Jazz Festival, a mountain retreat, or simply to soak in the charm of the Swiss Riviera, this is your perfect base.

The apartment blends contemporary comfort with timeless elegance — high ceilings, designer furnishings, a fully equipped kitchen, and a terrace where you can sip your morning coffee as the lake shimmers below.
